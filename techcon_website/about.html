History

TechCon was founded in 2001 as a small gathering of tech enthusiast. over the years, it has grown into one of the largest and most influential tech conferences in the world.

Mission

our misssion is to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning within the Technology community. we aim to bring together industry leaders, visionaries, and practitioners to share knowledge and drive the future of technology.
